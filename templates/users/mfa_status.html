About Two-Factor Authentication

Two-factor authentication (2FA) adds an extra layer of security to your account by requiring both your password and a time-based code from your mobile device.

Benefits

  • Protects your account even if your password is compromised
  • Prevents unauthorized access from unknown devices
  • Required for administrative and moderation roles
  • Industry standard for sensitive accounts

Supported Apps

  • Google Authenticator (Free)
  • Authy (Free, with backup)
  • Microsoft Authenticator (Free)
  • 1Password (Premium)
