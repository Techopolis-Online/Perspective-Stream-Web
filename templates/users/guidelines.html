Community Guidelines

Welcome to the Beyond the Gallery community! To ensure a positive and safe environment for everyone, please adhere to the following guidelines.

1. General Conduct

We expect all members to treat each other with respect and kindness. Harassment, hate speech, personal attacks, or any form of discrimination will not be tolerated. Engage in constructive discussions and help foster a welcoming atmosphere.

  • Be Respectful: Always be courteous and considerate towards other members, even when you disagree.
  • No Harassment: Do not engage in any form of harassment, bullying, or intimidation.
  • Privacy: Respect the privacy of others. Do not share personal information without consent.
  • Stay on Topic: Keep discussions relevant to the community's purpose â€“ sharing and discussing iOS shortcuts.

2. Content Guidelines

The content you share, including shortcuts, comments, and reviews, must comply with these guidelines. We encourage creativity and innovation, but all submissions must be appropriate and safe for all users.

  • Legal & Ethical: All shared content must be legal and ethical. Do not share shortcuts that promote illegal activities, distribute malware, or infringe on intellectual property rights.
  • Appropriate Content: Content should be suitable for a general audience. No explicit, violent, or otherwise offensive material.
  • Accuracy: Ensure that descriptions and information provided for your shortcuts are accurate and truthful.
  • No Spam: Do not post unsolicited advertisements, chain messages, or repetitive content.

3. Reporting Violations

If you encounter content or behavior that violates these guidelines, please report it to our moderation team. Your reports help us maintain a healthy community.

  • How to Report: Use the designated reporting features available on the platform.
  • Provide Details: When reporting, provide as much detail as possible to help us investigate effectively.
  • Do Not Retaliate: Do not engage with or retaliate against users you believe have violated guidelines. Let the moderation team handle it.

4. Moderation and Enforcement

Our moderation team is committed to enforcing these guidelines fairly and consistently. Violations may result in content removal, temporary suspension, or permanent banning from the platform.

  • Discretion: Moderation decisions are made at the discretion of the Beyond the Gallery team.
  • Appeals: If you believe a moderation decision was made in error, you may appeal by contacting us through the designated channels.

These guidelines are subject to change. Please check back periodically for updates.
